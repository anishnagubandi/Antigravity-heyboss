# Recovery Supervision Service - Registration UI Design

## 🎨 Color Palette
- **Primary (Action/Buttons):** #4CAF50 (Green)  
- **Secondary (Highlights/Headers):** #2196F3 (Blue)  
- **Background:** #F5F5F5 (Light Gray)  
- **Text (Primary):** #212121 (Dark Gray/Black)  
- **Text (Secondary/Labels):** #757575 (Medium Gray)  
- **Consent/Warning Notes:** #FF9800 (Orange)

## 🎨 UI Styling Notes
- **Form Background:** Light gray (#F5F5F5) for a clean, neutral look.  
- **Input Fields:** White background with subtle border (#CCCCCC).  
- **Labels:** Medium gray (#757575) for readability.  
- **Buttons:** Green (#4CAF50) with white text for "Register".  
- **Hover State:** Darker green (#388E3C).  
- **Consent Section:** Orange (#FF9800) accent to draw attention.
